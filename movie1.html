Avatar

On the lush alien world of Pandora live the Na'vi, beings who appear primitive but are highly evolved. Because the planet's environment is poisonous, these aliens are called Avatars...

They must link with human minds in order to maintain movement on Pandora.Are you ready to see the adventures of the Avatars and their planet Pandora?!

Jake Sully who was paralyzed is able to walk again after one touch from an Avatar and ends up falling in love with her he then has to figure out how to survive in her world
